________Robot Elevator_______

If currentFloor == desiredFloor
	exit elevator
Else
	move to button panel

pressButton(desiredFloor)
Repeat





_____Navigating a 4 way stop____

If stopped at 4 way stop
	If other cars are present == False
		Drive through.
	Else if car is to your right
		Wait for car to drive through then drive through
	Else if car is to your left or opposite you
		Drive through



_____putting away groceries_____

While groceries are on counter
	If item is perishible place in fridge
	Else if item is spice place in spice cupboard
	Else if item is canned place in cans cupboard
	Else place item in dry goods cupboard
